Action-First UI

Core Features

What We'll Build

Not a generic restaurant template — a mobile-first website built specifically for how The Backyard Place attracts and serves customers in Kalibo.

Homepage with Bento Grid

A visually rich homepage showcasing hero imagery, menu highlights, location, hours (10 AM – 12 MN daily), and trust signals — all in a modern bento grid layout.

Shareable Digital Menu

A dedicated menu page customers can share via Messenger and social media — complete with categories, prices, and mouth-watering photos.

Mobile-First + Click-to-Call

Built for how Filipinos browse — fast on mobile data, with a sticky call button (0951 130 6264) always visible so customers can reserve instantly.

SEO & Google Maps

Local SEO so you rank for "seafood restaurant Kalibo" and "grill bar near Boracay." Embedded Google Maps to Old Buswang, Kalibo, Aklan.

Facebook & Messenger Integration

Direct links to your Facebook page (@backyardkalibo), Messenger chat widget, and social sharing — connecting your online presence seamlessly.

Deployment & Hosting
